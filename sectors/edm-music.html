01 / What The System Does

A music intelligence engine tracking the electronic music landscape and generating production direction

The EDM intelligence pipeline scans the electronic music ecosystem across three dimensions. The market dimension tracks streaming data, playlist placements, chart movements, and label release schedules across Spotify, Apple Music, Beatport, SoundCloud, and Bandcamp. The event dimension monitors festival lineups, club bookings, tour announcements, and venue programming across 500+ venues and 200+ festivals globally. The production dimension analyzes trending sounds, BPM ranges, key signatures, and production techniques across genre subcategories.

For each weekly cycle, the system generates production intelligence: which sounds are emerging, which are peaking, and which are declining. It identifies cross-genre pollination patterns and generates AI music generation cues with specific BPM, key, instrumentation, texture, and arrangement parameters that can be fed directly into tools like Suno, Udio, or Ableton-integrated AI plugins.

04 / Use Cases

Specific scenarios where the system changes the outcome

Scenario 01

A producer needs to identify the next emerging sub-genre before it peaks

The system tracks cross-pollination patterns: when melodic techno producers start incorporating breaks at 138 BPM with UK garage vocal chops, it flags the convergence. Streaming data confirms acceleration. The producer receives specific production parameters and releases into the trend 6-8 weeks before saturation.

Scenario 02

A record label needs A&R intelligence to identify unsigned artists gaining momentum

The system scans SoundCloud uploads, Spotify algorithmic playlist additions, and social media growth rates. When an unsigned artist hits velocity thresholds, the system generates a complete artist profile. The label contacts artists 30-60 days before competing labels notice.

Scenario 03

A festival promoter needs to optimize their lineup for ticket sales

The system analyzes streaming data, social media reach, touring history, and market saturation. It generates a lineup optimization model maximizing unique audience reach while minimizing conflicts and optimizing artist fee ROI.

Scenario 04

An AI music company needs to understand gaps between listener preferences and model capabilities

The system identifies trending music characteristics that AI tools struggle to replicate and quantifies the gap with technical specifications for improvement.
