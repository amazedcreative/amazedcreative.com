01 / What The System Does

A regulatory intelligence engine that tracks every rule change, guidance update, and enforcement action affecting your business

The compliance pipeline monitors the regulatory environment across federal, state, and industry-specific channels. The federal layer scans the Federal Register daily for proposed rules, final rules, and notices from every agency. The state layer monitors regulatory databases across configured states for rule changes, enforcement actions, and guidance updates. The industry layer tracks sector-specific requirements: CMMC for defense contractors, HIPAA for healthcare, SOX for public companies, PCI for payment processors. The enforcement layer monitors agency inspection schedules, consent decrees, and penalty assessments.

The system maps every regulatory change to your specific compliance obligations. When a new rule is proposed, the system identifies which of your operations are affected, estimates the compliance cost and timeline, and generates an action plan. When enforcement patterns shift, the system alerts before the inspectors arrive. Average early warning: 90-180 days before a final rule takes effect, giving your compliance team time to prepare instead of react.

03 / What You Get

A weekly compliance brief with regulatory changes, enforcement signals, and action items

Weekly Brief

Delivered every Monday. Contains: new regulatory changes affecting your operations mapped to compliance obligations, enforcement action summaries in your industry, upcoming compliance deadlines with preparation checklists, agency inspection and examination priorities, and public comment period tracking for proposed rules you should influence.

Alert Types

  • New Rule — Final rule published affecting your operations. Compliance requirements, timeline, and estimated cost.
  • Enforcement Shift — Agency enforcement priorities change in your area. Inspection preparation recommendations.
  • Comment Period — Proposed rule in public comment period. Impact analysis and recommended comment strategy.
  • Deadline Approaching — Compliance deadline within 90 days. Preparation checklist and gap analysis.
  • Penalty Alert — Significant enforcement penalty in your industry. Compliance gap assessment triggered.
04 / Use Cases

Specific scenarios where the system changes the outcome

Scenario 01

A defense contractor needs to track CMMC compliance requirements across 200+ controls

The system monitors every CMMC-related update from DoD, NIST, and the CMMC Accreditation Body. When control interpretations change or new assessment guidance is published, the system maps it to the contractor's specific implementation. Gap analysis updates automatically. The contractor maintains continuous compliance instead of scrambling before assessments.

Scenario 02

A healthcare system monitors CMS rule changes across 12 hospitals in 4 states

The system tracks federal CMS rules and state Medicaid program changes simultaneously. When a rule change affects reimbursement rates, documentation requirements, or quality metrics, the system identifies every affected facility, estimates the financial impact, and generates a compliance implementation plan. The health system avoids $2.3M in potential penalties in the first year.

Scenario 03

A financial institution needs to track regulatory changes across 7 federal agencies and 3 states

The system monitors SEC, FDIC, OCC, CFPB, FinCEN, OFAC, and state banking departments. Overlapping and conflicting requirements are flagged. When a new regulation creates compliance gaps, the system generates a prioritized remediation plan with effort estimates and deadlines.
