01 / What The System Does

A grants intelligence engine that finds every funding opportunity you qualify for and helps you apply

The grants pipeline scans the entire funding landscape — federal, state, foundation, and corporate giving. The discovery layer monitors Grants.gov (2,000+ open opportunities at any time), foundation IRS 990 filings, state funding portals, and corporate giving program announcements. The matching layer scores each opportunity against your organization's mission, capabilities, geographic focus, and budget range. The analysis layer extracts application requirements, evaluation criteria, and historical award patterns. The support layer generates application outlines, budget templates, and narrative frameworks.

For each matching grant, the system provides: complete application requirements, historical award data (who won, how much, for what), evaluation criteria with weight distributions, and a preliminary application outline. Organizations discover 3-5x more eligible opportunities than manual searching, and application preparation time drops by 40% through automated requirement extraction and outline generation.

03 / What You Get

A weekly grants brief with new opportunities, deadline tracking, and application intelligence

Weekly Brief

Delivered every Monday. Contains: new grant opportunities matching your eligibility profile, upcoming deadlines within 60 days with preparation status, historical award analysis for targeted grants, foundation giving trend analysis, and application preparation checklists for priority opportunities.

Alert Types

  • Grant Match — New funding opportunity matching your eligibility criteria. Full NOFO analysis and application requirements.
  • Deadline Alert — Application deadline approaching for a tracked opportunity. Preparation checklist and gap analysis.
  • Award Pattern — Historical award data reveals strategic insight for a targeted grant program. Win strategy adjustment recommended.
  • Foundation Shift — Tracked foundation changes giving priorities. Impact on your pipeline and recommended pivots.
  • New Program — Federal agency announces new grant program in your area. Early application strategy recommended.
04 / Use Cases

Specific scenarios where the system changes the outcome

Scenario 01

A nonprofit needs to diversify funding across 20+ grant sources

The system identifies every eligible federal, state, and foundation grant matching the organization's mission. It generates a 12-month application calendar prioritized by amount, win probability, and strategic alignment. The organization submits 35 applications in the first year (up from 12) and increases grant revenue by 180%.

Scenario 02

A university research lab tracks NIH and NSF funding announcements

The system monitors program announcements, study section compositions, and funding rate trends. When a new RFA aligns with the lab's research, the system generates an application outline within 24 hours, including suggested specific aims based on the funding agency's priorities and the lab's publication history.

Scenario 03

A local government needs to maximize federal infrastructure funding

The system tracks IIJA, IRA, and other federal infrastructure programs. It matches the municipality's project priorities to eligible programs, identifies matching fund requirements, and generates application summaries for each opportunity. The city captures $12M in federal funds it would have missed without systematic tracking.
