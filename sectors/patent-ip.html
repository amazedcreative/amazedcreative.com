01 / What The System Does

A patent intelligence engine tracking competitor filings, expirations, and IP landscape shifts in your technology domain

The patent pipeline monitors the entire intellectual property landscape for your technology domain. The filing layer scans USPTO, EPO, WIPO, and jurisdiction-specific patent offices for new applications, grants, and continuations. The citation layer maps the citation network — which patents cite which, creating a technology dependency graph. The expiration layer tracks patent term adjustments, maintenance fee payments, and terminal disclaimers to identify when protection windows close. The litigation layer monitors IPR proceedings, district court filings, and ITC investigations.

The system builds a complete IP landscape map for your technology area: who owns what, where the whitespace exists, which patents are expiring (creating market openings), and which competitors are building patent portfolios in specific technology directions. Patent application language analysis identifies competitors' R&D direction 18-24 months before products launch.

04 / Use Cases

Specific scenarios where the system changes the outcome

Scenario 01

A biotech company monitors competitor patent filings to predict product launches

The system tracks patent applications from 15 competitors. When a competitor files a series of applications around a specific mechanism of action, the system identifies the pattern 18 months before clinical trial announcements. The company adjusts its own R&D pipeline to avoid direct competition and identifies licensing opportunities.

Scenario 02

A semiconductor company tracks patent expirations to time generic product launches

The system monitors maintenance fee payments and patent term calculations for key semiconductor process patents. When a foundational patent is within 24 months of expiration, the system alerts with freedom-to-operate analysis, market size estimate, and competitive entry timeline.

Scenario 03

A patent licensing firm identifies undervalued portfolios for acquisition

The system tracks patent assignment records, maintenance fee lapses, and bankruptcy filings. When a technology company allows maintenance fees to lapse on patents with high citation counts, the system flags the acquisition opportunity with valuation analysis.
